About Me

I'm a frontend developer and designer based in Abuja, Nigeria, with a background in electrical engineering. I didn’t start in tech—my journey began in the renewable energy and CCTV space—but my passion for problem-solving and creativity led me into web development. I specialize in building intuitive, user-friendly applications with TypeScript, React, Tailwind CSS, and Styled Components. Whether it’s hardware, software, or even my cooking experiments, I’ve always had a knack for breaking things just to fix them—and that curiosity drives how I approach code. With a Post-Graduate Diploma in Electrical Engineering from FUTA, I now channel my skills into creating digital solutions that solve real-world problems and improve lives.
